Shot Tracker

An iOS app designed to help ice hockey goalies improve their performance in-game. Split between two use-cases, the first allows for coaches to place a goalie and a puck on the ice. The application calculates a series of metrics to better explain positioning. The second portion can be used by stat-trackers in the stands to track puck movement live, and replay it for goalies to view later. Sorted by outcome, this allows goalies to see common pitfalls in their game to tailor future practices.
